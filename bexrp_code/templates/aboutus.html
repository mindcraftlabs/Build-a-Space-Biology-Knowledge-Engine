2025 NASA Space Apps Challenge — Build a Space Biology Knowledge Engine

Event: 2025 NASA Space Apps Challenge

Enable a new era of human space exploration — click to expand for details.

NASA has performed biology experiments in space for decades, generating a large amount of data. Your challenge is to build a dynamic dashboard that leverages AI, knowledge graphs, and/or other tools to summarize NASA bioscience publications and enable exploration of experimental results.

Why the challenge matters — click to expand for background.

Exploring the Moon and Mars safely requires understanding how living systems respond to space. NASA's research provides valuable insights, but this data can be hard to navigate. AI and informatics approaches can organize and summarize this knowledge effectively.

Primary goals — click to expand for objectives.

  • Dynamic dashboard with interactive search features
  • AI-assisted summaries, keyword extraction, author discovery
  • Interactive knowledge graph to reveal progress and gaps
  • Downloadable metadata for analysis

Lightweight Flask app with Python backend for data ingestion, SQLite database, and frontend JavaScript modules for charts and knowledge graph visualization.

Key points: server-side article indexing and AI-friendly metadata extraction (via pmc_toolkit.py), API served by app.py, client-side charts and knowledgegraph.js.
